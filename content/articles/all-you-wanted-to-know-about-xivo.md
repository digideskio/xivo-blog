**DEFINITION: XIVO, A FREE IPBX**

-   XiVO is an unified communications software system licensed under the
    GPLv3,[GNU General Public
    License](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GNU_General_Public_License%20v3).
-   The graphical user interface (GUI) provides an interface for
    configuring, controlling, and managing the [IP
    PBX](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IP_PBX) software. The XiVO Distro
    includes packages that offer these features:[Voice over
    IP](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Voice_over_IP), PBX, Fax, voice
    mail and unified messaging. XiVO is based on the free software
    [Asterisk](https://fr.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asterisk_%28logiciel%29)
    and the XiVO Linux distribution is based on [Debian
    Wheezy](https://www.debian.org/releases/wheezy/index.fr.html).

**HISTORY ABOUT XIVO AND AVENCALL**

-   XiVO was created in 2005 as an open-source, collaborative project
    from
    [Proformatique](http://www.quebecentreprises.com/proformatique-inc-u2py),
    a company in France.
-   In 2010, [Avencall](http://www.avencall.com/en/), a French leader in
    the publishing and integration of open unified communications
    solutions, merged with Proformatique (XiVO). Avencall wanted to
    pursue a vision that would reinforce XiVO's strong points :
    technical development, commercial features, IT solutions. Avencall
    became the first provider of free VoIP solutions in France, with
    thousands of users deployed and 350 customers such as LDC, Groupama,
    la Poste, l'Olympique Lyonnais.
-   Avencall is also specialized in contact center solutions with its
    XiVO-CC open source software suite.
-   Avencall, based in France, has offices in Lyon (HQ), Paris,
    Toulouse, Rennes, Aix-en-Provence and Strasbourg. Most of its
    research and development is in Quebec-city, Canada.

**FEATURES**

-   XiVO provides online repositories that contain software packages.
    The latest release is documented and updated every 3 weeks. Access
    to the [documentation](http://xivo.readthedocs.org/en/stable).
-   Notable changes in the last release include : bookmarks for
    contacts, synchronisation between servers, management of function
    keys via REST API, new authentification and encryption framework,
    upgrade to [Asterisk
    13](/index.php?post/2015/07/20/XiVO-and-Asterisk-13).
